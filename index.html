Importance of Exercise

Exercise helps children stay active, healthy, and energetic. Daily physical activities improve both physical and mental health.

Activities such as running, football, cycling, dancing, and yoga help make the body stronger and healthier.

Quick Health Tips

  • Eat healthy fruits and vegetables
  • Drink enough water every day
  • Sleep early and take proper rest
  • Exercise regularly for good health
